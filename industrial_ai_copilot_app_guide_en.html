Application Overview

The Industrial AI Operations Copilot is a multi-page Streamlit application deployed on Snowflake (Container Runtime) that provides AI-powered operational intelligence across 8 pages, organized in 3 navigation groups.

🗂️ Navigation Structure

  • 📊 Operations
    • 📈 Operations Center — Plant-wide KPIs, charts, and AI executive summary
    • 🏗️ Production Plant — Hierarchical plant explorer with equipment detail
  • 🤖 Intelligence
    • 🚨 Alerts & Smart Actions — Kanban board with AI-powered investigation
    • 🎫 Ticket Assistant — AI ticket analysis with knowledge base search
    • 💬 Ask AI — Natural-language Q&A over operational data
  • 📅 Planning
    • 📦 Production Planner — Orders, rescheduling simulation, and AI planning
    • 🔧 Predictive Maintenance — Risk scoring with failure window predictions
    • 🚛 Fleet Maintenance Copilot — Operator complaint intake with AI diagnostics
Cortex AI (COMPLETE) mistral-large2 for summaries, diagnoses, recommendations, and multi-step reasoning
Cortex Search snowflake-arctic-embed-m-v1.5 semantic search across 35 technical manuals and tickets
Real-Time Data 2.8M+ sensor readings, 446 equipment, 1,470 sensors, 966 alerts, 452 work orders
6 Cortex UDFs Summarize, classify, extract entities, troubleshoot, disposition, reschedule

Multi-Step Reasoning Intent classification → SQL generation → data retrieval → KB search → answer synthesis
Structured + Unstructured Combines SQL query results with Cortex Search evidence for grounded answers
Transparency Shows retrieved data table, evidence consulted (tickets/manuals), and the SQL query used

User asks: "Which production line is limiting performance?" Multiple lines under maintenance visible (LINE-31-1, LINE-31-2, LINE-16-1, LINE-47-1).

AI answer identifies LINE-31-1 in the Packaging zone of Plant B17 as the performance bottleneck due to its low throughput of 53 KG/HR and maintenance status. Key findings: lowest throughput, currently in maintenance, ticket indicating issues with packaging line conveyor CV-401.
